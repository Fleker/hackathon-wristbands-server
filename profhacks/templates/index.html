What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is like a weekend science fair, student teams come to build cool things that are then judged. Winners can take home a lot of awesome prizes!

Rowan's Inaugural Hackathon

Rowan's IEEE (Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is hosting its inaugural hackathon at Rowan University!

Rowan University is located in Glassboro, NJ, just half an hour away from Philadelphia. The event will be hosted in Rowan Hall, the engineering building. Participants can park for free in Lot D, just off of Joseph L. Bowe Blvd.

Enter the Physical Web.

Our general theme is the physical web, how the Internet and technology interface with the world around us. There are three different distinct categories of the physical web. The best hack in each category will receive an award in addition to 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.

Electrical & Computer Engineering at Rowan gives students many opportunities to study the physical web, whether classes in digital signal processing or smart buildings, or clinic projects in the virtual reality lab and other applications.

Quantified Self

Wearable technology gives us more data about our vitals than ever before. How does tracking our steps, location, and heart rate enable us to have greater insights about our health?

Sensor Journalism

By collecting data using a network of sensors, you are able to measure and predict patterns about our environment. How can you use data to tell a story?

Smart Buildings

With the Internet of Things, our thermostat, smoke alarm, and lightbulbs can talk to our phones and to each other. How does this greater connectivity allow our home to work on our behalf?

Not your average game...

Along with your registration to ProfHacks, you will receive a ticket for a tour of a futuristic Mars research base. At a predetermied time, you and five other hackers can go to room 239 to begin. However, while you are touring, there may be an... 'incident' that will leave you trapped.

Escape rooms are a popular type of ARG (augmented reality game) which require players to team up and solve puzzles in order to escape a room in the fastest possible time. Our escape room, which has been planned and built entirely by Rowan students, is an optional mini event that hackers can do as a break.

The top three fastest times by the end of the hackathon will win prizes.

We are partnering with MLH (Major League Hacking) to provide the best possible experience to participants. By attending this event, you agree to follow the MLH Code of Conduct.
